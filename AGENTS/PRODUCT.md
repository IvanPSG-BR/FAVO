# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Stardew Valley players and "advanced farmers" who want to manage their in-game finances with more depth than the vanilla game provides. They are looking for efficiency and a sense of progression.

## Product Purpose
A pseudo agro-finance manager (FAVO) designed to help players track transactions, optimize crop profits, and manage farm assets. It bridges the gap between a cozy game and a serious financial tool.

## Brand Personality
Cozy, Efficient, Wealthy. A mix of "earth-under-nails" grit and "overflowing-silos" prosperity.

## Anti-references
Corporate bank apps. Avoid cold, sterile, blue-heavy interfaces. No generic SaaS "cream and pastel" palettes that lack character.

## Design Principles
- **Rustic Precision**: High-utility dashboard layouts wrapped in cozy, farm-inspired textures and tones.
- **Golden Harvest**: Gold is used as a reward and highlight, signifying growth and the value of the player's labor.
- **Grounded Stability**: Deep browns provide the foundation, evoking the soil and a sense of long-term security.
- **Organic Clarity**: Typography and spacing that feel natural and breathable, avoiding the cramped feeling of spreadsheets.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain WCAG AA contrast standards, especially for financial data. Support reduced motion for game-like transitions.
